3-2-1 Backup Strategy

Industry-leading backup methodology ensuring maximum data protection and business continuity

3

Copies of Data

Maintain three complete copies of all critical data across different storage systems and locations for maximum redundancy.

2

Different Media

Store data on two different types of media to protect against media-specific failures and ensure diverse recovery options.

1

Offsite Location

Keep at least one backup copy in an offsite location to protect against local disasters and geographic risks.

Infrastructure Components

🏗️

Local RAID Protection

Redundant storage arrays providing immediate protection against drive failures with hot-swap capabilities.

  • RAID 5 (Active Server) - Performance + Redundancy
  • RAID 1 (Backup Server) - Mirror Protection
  • Hot-swappable drive bays
  • Real-time health monitoring
📸

Intelligent Snapshots

Automated snapshot system with variable frequency based on data criticality and change patterns.

  • Critical files: Every 3 hours
  • Standard files: Every 24 hours
  • Point-in-time recovery
  • Incremental snapshot technology
🌍

Offsite Backup Network

Geographically distributed backup infrastructure across multiple continents for maximum protection.

  • Hong Kong data center
  • Toronto, Canada facility
  • Energy-efficient scheduling
  • Encrypted transmission
🔍

Backup Verification

Comprehensive testing and monitoring systems ensuring backup integrity and recoverability.

  • Monthly restore testing
  • Automated integrity checks
  • Performance monitoring
  • Alert notifications
